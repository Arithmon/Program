**Statement.** Promote the explicit Donaldson-type near-solution on K7 (K3
fibration over S3, Z2^3 fiber symmetry) to an exact torsion-free G2 metric,
or characterize the obstruction.

**Why it matters.** This is the program's central lock. The topology
(21, 77) is rigorous, the Calabi-Yau residual is interval-certified, the
near-solution is explicit; exactness is what separates "a certified candidate"
from "the geometry".

**Known constraints.** Existence by gluing (Joyce-Kovalev type) is guaranteed
for small enough parameter, but a closed-form exact representative is
research-level. Blockwise Newton-Kantorovich contractibility near the
near-solution has been verified on all 7 nontrivial Z2^3 isotypic blocks
(margins 7x). Four classical construction templates (Joyce, TCS, extra-twisted
TCS, CCY7) are excluded by theorem; the object is not covered by existing
taxonomies.

**Next step.** Full adiabatic coupling across blocks (Stage 2 of the
Kantorovich program); singular Monge-Ampere and continuity-method routes
scoped as alternatives.
